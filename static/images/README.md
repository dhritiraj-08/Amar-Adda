# Background Images Guide

Upload your background images to this folder (`static/images/`) with these specific names:

## Required Image Files:
- `about-bg.jpg` - Background for About Us section
- `work-bg.jpg` - Background for Our Work section  
- `services-bg.jpg` - Background for Services section
- `team-bg.jpg` - Background for Our Team section
- `contact-bg.jpg` - Background for Contact section

## Image Requirements:
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 or higher (Full HD recommended)
- **File size**: Keep under 2MB each for faster loading
- **Aspect ratio**: 16:9 works best

## Image Content Suggestions:
- **about-bg.jpg**: Team collaboration, creative workspace, or Assam landscape
- **work-bg.jpg**: Event footage, production stills, or project highlights
- **services-bg.jpg**: Equipment setup, studio shots, or service delivery
- **team-bg.jpg**: Team photos, behind-the-scenes, or group activities
- **contact-bg.jpg**: Office space, contact methods, or communication theme

## Current Setup:
- Images will be automatically applied as section backgrounds
- A dark overlay will be added for text readability
- Images will be responsive and cover the full section area

Once you upload the images with the correct names, they will automatically appear as backgrounds for each section!
